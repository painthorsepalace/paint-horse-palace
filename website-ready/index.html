Welcome

A spacious retreat where comfort, fun & nature come together

Surrounded by tall pines, Paint Horse Palace offers peaceful privacy while keeping you minutes from local dining, shops, wineries, and attractions — and just a short drive from Broken Bow Lake and Beavers Bend State Park. Inside, a bright great room with natural pine walls, stone accents, and large windows creates a warm, inviting atmosphere. Whether you're here to relax or to adventure, this is your ideal home base in Hochatown.
